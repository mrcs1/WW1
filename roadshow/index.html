WW1 Road Show
Trinity College Dublin
July 12, 2014
↓

RTÉ's World War 1 season began with a major Road Show in Trinity College Dublin on Saturday 12th July, 2014.

The WW1 Road Show launched the beginning of a major national retrospective marking the 100th anniversary of World War 1. A day-long programme of events attended by more than 8000 people, included lectures, pop-up talks, family events, exhibitions and a memorabilia collection day, where members of the public could bring along items relating to World War 1 to be catalogued for an online European archive.
