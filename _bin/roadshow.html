WW1 Road Show
Trinity College Dublin
July 12, 2014
↓

The WW1 Road Show at Trinity College Dublin is part of a major retrospective to mark the centenary of World War 1. This day-long programme of free events includes pop up talks, WW1 cooking demonstrations, music, theatre, lectures and family activities.

You can find a full listing of events on the Trinity College Dublin website

Members of the public were invited to bring along items relating to World War 1 to be catalogued for an online European archive at the Family Memorabilia event. Experts from the National Library of Ireland were be on hand to review and record family memorabilia.
